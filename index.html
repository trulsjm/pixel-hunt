How to play
>
The controls for this game are easy:
Walk with the arrow keys.
Tap space to interact with people or objects.
And tap M to open your menu, where your bag, money and pokedex is at.
You can press S to save game, or click the button in your menu.
Buttons on-screen are to be clicked with the cursor.
